ABOUT ME

Hello There!
My interest in Information Technology started early, thanks to my brother’s love for computers. I was fascinated by watching him play games like Doom and Duke Nukem on his old Pentium 1. Those days spent around floppy disks and clunky monitors ignited my lasting passion for tech.

In 2020, I took my first significant step into the IT world with building my own PC. After nearly 12 hours of assembly, the thrill of seeing my custom-built system fire up perfectly was incredible. This hands-on project, which included a Prime Z490-A motherboard, Intel i9-10850K processor, NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060, and a Lian Li O11D XL-W case, was a turning point. It marked the start of my deep dive into tech, from tweaking settings for gaming to managing hardware components. Even though I had a successful career in retail management, my love for IT never faded. Juggling management duties left me little time for formal IT education, but by mid-2022, I knew it was time to make a change. In December 2023, I left my role as an assistant manager to fully commit to IT, focusing on gaining experience and earning certifications.
